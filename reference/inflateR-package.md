# inflateR: Inflation Adjustment for Historical Currency Values

Convert historical monetary values into their present-day equivalents
using bundled CPI (Consumer Price Index) and GDP deflator data sourced
from the World Bank Development Indicators. Supports British pounds
(GBP), Australian dollars (AUD), US dollars (USD), Euro (EUR), Canadian
dollars (CAD), Japanese yen (JPY), Chinese yuan (CNY), Swiss francs
(CHF), New Zealand dollars (NZD), Indian rupees (INR), South Korean won
(KRW), Brazilian reais (BRL), and Norwegian krone (NOK). Currency codes
and country names are both accepted as input.
